How to Play 🎵

Match musical symbols, hit your objectives, and score as highly as you can before time or moves run out.

  • 🎮 Swap adjacent tiles to make a match of 3 or more.
  • 📱 On mobile, swipe a tile in the direction you want it to move.
  • 🎯 Complete the level objectives shown above the board before you run out of moves or time.
  • 🎼 Each level is different and may ask you to collect specific instruments.
  • 🏆 Bigger matches score more, and combos or chain reactions give bonus points.
  • 💡 Plan carefully and use your moves wisely to build your best score.

Good luck and have fun building your best score! ✨
